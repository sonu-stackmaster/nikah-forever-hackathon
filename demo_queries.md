Here are some example queries you can try with QueryGPT:

### English

- "Show me active users by city"
- "What's the gender distribution of our users?"
- "How many users joined this year?"
- "Which cities have the most female users?"

## 💡 Tips for Better Queries

1. **Be Specific**: Include time frames, filters, or specific metrics
2. **Use Natural Language**: Ask as you would ask a human analyst
3. **Mix Languages**: Feel free to use Hindi/English combinations
4. **Ask Follow-ups**: Drill down into interesting findings
5. **Request Visualizations**: Ask for "trends", "breakdown", or "comparison"
